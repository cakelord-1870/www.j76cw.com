J76 Bamboo Wear

Founded since 2003, in the heart of Vancouver, Canada. J76 Bamboo & Athletic Wear is a yoga apparel company with a retail philosophy of simplicity, purity, and serenity based in Vancouver. We manufacture the majority of our products locally in the Lower Mainland, to ensure the highest quality and use only environmentally sourced and natural bamboo wear.

Feather Lite and Leostar are our active wear line and along with J76, our yoga apparel, were created for pure comfort, long lasting durability, and represent an ideal blend style and optimum performance.

Durable active wear made for a wide variety of fitness levels and activities. Perfect for indoor and outdoor sports. Certain items include UV protection and all items are moisture wicking and shrink/fade resistant.
